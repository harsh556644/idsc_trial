The Standard.

Born out of frustration. Built for excellence. We are redefining the diplomatic circuit.

The Problem

A Broken Circuit.

For too long, the regional Model UN circuit has been plagued by unaccountable organizers, last-minute cancellations, withheld refunds, and biased judging. Delegates invest their time and money only to be met with unprofessionalism. The ecosystem was failing its brightest minds.

Our Mission

Restoring Integrity.

IDSC was founded to be the ultimate regulatory body. We audit conferences, enforce strict financial and procedural guidelines, and provide a secure grievance platform for delegates. If an event carries the IDSC seal, it means it is a premium, verified diplomatic experience.

Core Values.

Accountability

Zero tolerance for logistical failures and financial mismanagement by organizers.

Transparency

Clear rubrics, open grievance tracking, and verified conference audits.

Excellence

Elevating the standard of debate through world-class training and masterclasses.
